P10S-C/4L
=========
Quad LAN with Platinum Grade Power Saving Technology
* Intel LGA1151 socket
* Intel C232 chipset
* 1x mini-SAS HD port (4x SATA 6Gb/s)
* 4 DIMM up to 64GB, DDR4 2133 ECC/non-ECC UDIMMM
* 2x M.2 Socket, up to 2280
* Dual M.2 build RAID0 for better performance
* PCI support extend life cycle for legacy device
* Quad Intel I210 Server Class GbE delivering high networking bandwidth
* Platinum-grade power efficiency with ultra longevity solid CAP & Beat Thermal Choke III

Z9PA-U8
=======
cpu:        E5-2600 and E5-1600 v2 product family
socket:     1x Socket 2011
core logic: Intel C602-A PCH
ram:        8x DIMM (max 256GB)
slots:      7x total (1x PCI 32, 3x PCI-E x8, 2x PCI-E x16,
            1x MIO for audio card)
sata:       4 x SATA2 3Gb/s ports
            2 x SATA3 6Gb/s ports
i/o ports:  1 x VGA Port
            2 x RJ-45 + 1 x Mgmt LAN
            NOTE: No eSATA port
size:       ATX-sized (12"x9.6), (EEB mounting hole locations)
